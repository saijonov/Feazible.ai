Our 3-Step Process to AI-Powered Bookkeeping

Our AI-powered solution precisely extracts, formats, and imports your invoices from a variety of applications into QuickBooks with 100% accuracy. Say goodbye to data entry errors and focus on what really matters—growing your business.

01

Understand & Analyze

  • We start by understanding your unique bookkeeping challenges and reviewing the applications you currently use.
  • From there, we map out the data flow between your systems, ensuring no detail is overlooked.
02

Build & Train

  • We create custom code that integrates all your applications, using advanced techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), vector embeddings, and API integration.
  • With these methods, we teach the AI to understand your specific needs, ensuring it formats and processes data exactly the way you want.
  • If needed Your data remains secure throughout, hosted on encrypted servers to protect sensitive information.
03

Understand & Analyze

  • Once trained, we integrate the AI with your system, either directly into your existing software or through a user-friendly interface we provide.
  • Choose to let us manage the back end, or use your own site to access and upload files—it's completely up to you.

Case Study: Elevating Customer Support for a Financial Services Firm

Client Background

A retail chain with 15 locations, managing in-store sales, online orders via Shopify, and subscription services through Stripe, struggled with bookkeeping.

Our Solution

We built a custom integration using AI to automate data imports from their systems into QuickBooks, ensuring accuracy and secure data handling.

The Problem

  • Data Discrepancies: Integrating data from various platforms into QuickBooks led to mismatched records.
  • Manual Entry: Hours were spent manually correcting invoices, leading to frequent errors.
  • Multi-Location Confusion: Tracking sales from multiple stores was messy and inaccurate.
  • Failed Solutions: Previous integration of none AI tools couldn't handle their complex needs.

Results

  • Time Saved: Manual entry was eliminated, freeing up staff.
  • Accurate Data: Real-time, error-free records across all platforms.
  • Simplified Reporting: Easy tracking of store performance and overall finances.
  • Better Customer Experience: Automated billing reduced issues with subscriptions.
